Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement for Nursery admission?

For Nursery admission, your child must be 3 years old by September 30th of the admission year. Please refer to our detailed age criteria for other grades.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, Harmony Academy provides a safe and reliable transportation service covering various routes. Details regarding routes and fees are available upon request.

What documents are required for admission?

Typically, we require birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), passport-sized photographs, and identity proof of parents. A complete list can be found in our Admission Requirements section.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention and effective learning for every student.
